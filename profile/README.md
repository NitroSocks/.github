# NitroSocks - Next-Generation Secure Communication

## Who We Are

NitroSocks is an open-source organization dedicated to advancing the state of cybersecurity and privacy-preserving technologies. We believe in building a future where secure communication is accessible, reliable, and resilient against evolving threats.

## Our Mission

**To redefine secure communication in untrusted environments** by developing protocols and tools that provide genuine end-to-end protection, even when underlying transport layers are compromised.

## Core Focus Areas

- **Post-Quantum Cryptography** - Developing encryption that remains secure in the age of quantum computing
- **Protocol Obfuscation** - Creating communication channels resistant to detection and blocking
- **Zero-Trust Architectures** - Building systems that verify every interaction, trust no one by default
- **Adversarial Resilience** - Engineering protocols that remain secure even when underlying infrastructure is compromised

## Our Approach

We tackle security challenges through:
- **Protocol-level innovation** rather than application-layer patches
- **Defense in depth** with multiple layers of cryptographic protection
- **Continuous evolution** to address emerging threats and vulnerabilities
- **Community-driven development** that benefits from diverse perspectives
